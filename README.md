Customer Segmentation RFM Analysis Dashboard

​📌 Project Overview

​This project focuses on building an interactive, data-driven Customer Segmentation Dashboard using Power BI to perform a comprehensive RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis. The core objective of this analysis is to evaluate historical sales data, segment customers based on their purchasing behavior, and deliver actionable insights that help businesses maximize retention and reduce customer churn.

​By categorizing customer profiles, this dashboard empowers marketing and sales teams to identify high-value loyalists, spot seasonal purchasing trends, and implement targeted re-engagement strategies for at-risk segments.

​📊 Key Dashboard Features

​Executive KPI Summary: Displays high-level critical metrics at a glance, including total unique customer counts, overall revenue generated ($91.83M), overall average recency, and cumulative purchase frequency (100K).

​Recency Analysis: A vertical column chart highlighting the Average of Recency by Customer Segment to pinpoint exactly how long it has been since specific groups last interacted with the business.

​Customer Distribution: A horizontal bar chart mapping out the Count of CustomerID by Customer Segment, allowing stakeholders to easily visualize the volume of customers sitting within groups like "Recent Newbies," "About to Sleep," or "Hibernating".

​Monetary Value Contribution: An interactive Treemap analyzing the relationship between total revenue contribution and customer volume across segments, immediately isolating the highest revenue-driving behavior.

​Built-in Cross-Filtering Interactivity: Built with a balanced, scannable layout where clicking on any specific segment folder within the Treemap dynamically filters the entire page to update related counts, recency, and total transaction values on the fly.

​🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

​Power BI Desktop: Data modeling, visualization, and canvas layout optimization.

​DAX & Time Intelligence: Leveraged behind-the-scenes to structure transaction date hierarchies and summarize high-volume fields.

​Data Cleaning & Transformation: Handled structural formatting to group complex transaction data into readable behavioral classifications.

​ Business Impact

​Churn Prevention: Isolates slipping customer segments (e.g., "Hibernating / Drifting" or "About to Sleep") to allow teams to push automated win-back campaigns.

​Loyalty Optimization: Clearly identifies "Champions" and "High-Value" tiers to understand what behaviors drive long-term customer success and sustained engagement.
